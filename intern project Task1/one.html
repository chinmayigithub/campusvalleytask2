About the Book
Title: The Great Book of Chetan Bhagat "INDIA POSITIVE"

The "INDIA POSITIVE" Book of Chetan Bhagat is an amazing story about a balanced view of India's current state and future prospects, offering insights and ideas for fostering positive change.

Author: Chetan Bhagat

A prominent Indian author known for his bestselling novels..

Genre: Non-Fiction

The India Positive Book falls into the Non-fiction genre, "India Positive Book" aims to inspire readers to take a more optimistic and proactive approach towards building a better future for India
